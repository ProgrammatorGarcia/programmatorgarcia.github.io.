About Me

I am Mr. Garcia, the 7th grade Pre-Algebra teacher and the 12th grade Logic & Computation teacher. I graduated from the University of Texas at Rio Grande Valley with a Bachelor's of Computer Science with a GPA of 3.95. Before I was a teacher, I worked as a senior software developer for various companies under a leasing company. I worked primarely on Android applications, utilizing the brand new language "Kotlin" to convert software applications for Android phones to a modern standard. While the work payed well, I never found any satisfaction in my career. It never felt that I was working towards any meaningful or fruitful end. However, what I truly did enjoy the most from my experience was instructing interns, and my position as a T.A. back in university. Teaching is my joy. So I dismissed my prospects in software development to become a full-time teacher. I am grateful for the opportunity provided at Veritas, and I can't wait to see what the future has in stock!

As for my novel, I have been working on it for over 5 years now (how time flies). The name of the novel is called "Causality", and it is a trilogy set in a deterministic universe that is at its final stages of experiencing total heat death. One star remains, and two dwindling worlds, the Gate and the Planes, struggle to coexist. The Gate is an orbiting Dyson Sphere that is on its last legs. It strips away the lingering warmth of the star, using its energy to power the last remaining replicators that keep the vital functions of this civilization operational. There, the Machine God Primus, Heart of the Gate, tends to his empire. The Planes are comprised of floating islands centering in an endless sky. There, longstanding families of Magi lead the people in a dark age of magic. The world is untamed and wild and yet it is but a mockery of what it was, long ago. The protagonist of the story is a powerful Magus named Lucian D'aureville, the man who brought the Planes into its eternal dark age. He has gained a powerful ability called a sorcery. His sorcery allows him to peer into the future by a couple of seconds. Uniquely, he can see every possibility simultaneously in his clairvoyance. Armed with knowledge he shouldn't be able to know, he can decide a new course of action, thus violating the principle of determinism of this world. He calls this phenomenon "The Uncertainty Principle", and it is the title of the first book. Before the book begins, Lucian is locked away deep in a forgotten cell, entrapped in the flesh of a demon as punishment for his crime against magic. Through the actions of another, Lucian is freed, and armed with this power, he aims to stop the death of his world by creating a ripple of change through extreme actions to fight against its prophecised end.
